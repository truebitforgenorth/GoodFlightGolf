About the Writer:

Charles Greenfield
Writing the game, one hole at a time.

I’ve been chasing golf balls — and stories — for as long as I can remember. From early mornings at my local muni to unforgettable rounds on some of the game’s great courses, golf has taught me patience, persistence, and a healthy respect for the rough. I write the way I play: with curiosity, a love for tradition, and just enough humor to keep it fun. Writing the game, one hole at a time.
