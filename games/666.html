How Six-Six-Six Works

Six-Six-Six is a golf game for four players where teams rotate every six holes. Holes 1 through 6 use one pairing, holes 7 through 12 switch partners, and holes 13 through 18 rotate again so that each player partners with every other player during the round.

This scorekeeper helps keep the format organized live on the course so your group can focus on the round instead of tracking pairings by hand.
