How Bingo Bango Bongo Works

Bingo Bango Bongo is a golf points game that works especially well for mixed-skill groups because players can earn points in different ways on every hole. Each hole has three available points: Bingo for first on the green, Bango for closest to the pin once all players are on the green, and Bongo for first in the hole.

This scorekeeper helps your group keep those point awards organized live on the course without relying on paper notes.
